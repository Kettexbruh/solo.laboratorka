History of computing

The history of computing is a journey from the first mechanical devices to modern computers. In Ancient Greece, the Antikythera mechanism was created (1st century BC), which is recognized as the first analog computer. In the 17th century, the first mechanical calculating machines of Pascal and Leibniz appeared. Modern technology is based on electronic and digital technologies that provide rapid processing of large amounts of data.
